3. CVR Estimation Accuracy

Problem Remains

Core enhancement affecting bid optimization, budget allocation, and pacing. CVR inaccuracy is a primary root cause of performance gaps.

Current Limitation

  • Uses historical moving averages
  • Can't account for: seasonality, price changes, competition, sparse data

Proposed: ML Model

  • Train on: seasonality, pricing, category benchmarks, semantic groupings, tens of other factors/ML features — a great chunk of which have been identified already
  • 3,000 accounts of training data available
Challenges: Requires ML expertise, R&D intensive (iterative), uncertain magnitude, data quality needs validation
